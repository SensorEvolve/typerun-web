Frequently asked questions

How do I generate a PDF?

Select a document type from the home screen, fill in the form (it pre-loads with sample data to guide you), then tap Generate PDF. Your document is built instantly, entirely on your device.

How do I save or share my PDF?

On the preview screen, tap the share icon in the top-right corner. iOS will open the standard Share Sheet — you can save to Files, send via Mail, AirDrop it, or share to any app that accepts PDFs.

Does TypeRun require an internet connection?

No. TypeRun works completely offline. Your documents are generated and stored entirely on your device. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

Will my data be saved if I close the app?

Yes. TypeRun automatically saves your form data as you type. When you reopen the app, everything is exactly where you left it — for all three document types.

How do I create an invoice?

Tap the Invoice card on the home screen. Fill in your company details, client information, and line items. TypeRun calculates subtotals, tax, and the total automatically as you type. Tap Generate PDF when ready.

Can I change the currency symbol on the invoice?

TypeRun uses your device's regional settings to determine the currency symbol. To change it, update your region in iOS Settings → General → Language & Region.

I purchased TypeRun on one device. Can I use it on another?

Yes. TypeRun is a standard App Store purchase — it's available on all devices signed into your Apple ID at no extra cost, just like any other paid app.

What iOS version does TypeRun require?

TypeRun requires iOS 14 or later.
